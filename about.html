About Kourken Kasbarian

Download CV

Hi there! My name is Kourken and I'm a WordPress developer with a passion for creating engaging and functional websites. With over 5 years of experience in the field, I've honed my skills in developing custom WordPress themes and websites, optimizing site speed and performance, and providing top-notch technical support.

But it's not just my technical skills that set me apart. I also have a strong attention to detail, excellent problem-solving skills, and a collaborative mindset that allows me to work effectively with clients and team members alike.

I believe in creating solutions that meet the unique needs of each client and strive to exceed expectations with every project I undertake, be it meeting the deadlines on time or going the extra mile and providing perfect support during and after delivering a project.

When I'm not working on WordPress projects or writing code in general, you'll find me in the gym 85% of the time, because I'm a fitness enthusiast, gym addict to be exact, and I believe that staying active is important for both physical and mental well-being away from screens. I'm also a musicaholic, you'll most likely find me blasting music on my earbuds almost everywhere. You might ask what about the other 15%, well, I might be hanging out with my friends somwehre nice in the city, taking random amazing photos as part of my hobby of photography. Also, there is a slight chance that I'm actually having a sleep, about 1% of the time to be exact.

I enjoy exploring new technologies and staying up to date with the latest trends in web development. I also enjoy learning and reading about a wide range of topics, from science and technology to fitness and space.

Whether I'm working on a website or pursuing my hobbies, I bring the same level of dedication and enthusiasm to everything I do.

A bit of history:

My love for technology goes way back since I was a kid, I loved anything tech, from phones to computers and everything in between. For web development in particular, it started about 7 years ago when I was researching of how the websites that we visit and check daily on our devices are built, from there I discovered HTML/CSS/JS and the amazing ways that you can bring a website to life by filling few files with code. I'd say and I still say that's a form of 'Art'. when I built the first version of my website and my first few personal projects while learning web development, I was so happy and motivated to go further.

Then I discovered PHP and WordPress, after that I knew that I'm starting a career out of all the knowledge I learned, self-taught everything. Since then, I worked part and full-time for different companies, worked on lots of projects, from business websites to e-commerce platforms, and have gained a deep understanding of WordPress's capabilities and potential. I also did many freelance projects and gained knowledge of how remote opportunities work.

In addition to WordPress development, I also offer front-end development, design to code conversion, and other related services to ensure that your website is optimized for performance, engagement, and user experience. From speed optimization and content creation to analytics and tracking, I have the skills and expertise to take your website to the next level.

Soft Skills:

One of my strongest soft skills is my ability to communicate complex technical concepts to clients and stakeholders in a clear and concise way. I understand that not everyone is familiar with the technical jargon of web development, and I strive to make the process as accessible and transparent as possible through communication.

In addition to my technical and communication skills, I like problem solving and enjoy tackling challenges, finding innovative solutions that meet the needs of my clients while also pushing the boundaries of what's possible in WordPress development. There were some needs or features in some of the projects that I thought might be impossible to implement, but hours of researching and trial and error usually helps you get forward with such challenges. Flexibility, passion and determination to what I create are some of the other soft skills that gets me excited to work on and deliver projects successfully.

Contact Me:

I'm open to job opportunities where I can contribute, learn and grow. If you have an opportunity that match my skills and experience, or want to hire me for a freelance project, don't hesitate to contact me. I usually reply within 1 business day.

So why wait? Contact me today to learn more about how I can help you create a website that is not only visually stunning but also highly functional and optimized for success. I look forward to working with you!
